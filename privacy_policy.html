Privacy Policy

Effective Date: June 2024

This privacy policy explains how AquaTime ("we", "our", or "us") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our mobile application.

1. Permissions We Request

  • Notifications (POST_NOTIFICATIONS): We request permission to send you reminders to drink water. You can choose to allow or deny this permission at any time in your device settings.
  • Exact Alarms (SCHEDULE_EXACT_ALARM): We request permission to schedule precise reminders, even if your device is idle, to ensure you never miss a hydration alert.
  • Receive Boot Completed (RECEIVE_BOOT_COMPLETED): This allows the app to restore your reminders after your device restarts. No personal data is accessed or transmitted.
  • Internet (INTERNET): Required for displaying ads and connecting to backend services (e.g., Firebase).

2. Data Collection and Usage

  • Local Storage: We store your reminder times, ad impression counts, onboarding status, and rating popup state locally on your device. This data is not transmitted to our servers.
  • Notifications: We use your device's notification system to remind you to drink water. No notification content is shared externally.
  • Ads: We use Google AdMob to display ads. AdMob may collect device information and use cookies or similar technologies as described in their Privacy Policy.
  • Analytics & Crash Reporting: We use Firebase, which may collect anonymized usage data and crash reports to improve app stability and performance. See Firebase Privacy and Security for more details.

3. Third-Party Services

  • Google AdMob: Used for displaying ads. AdMob may collect and use data as described in their privacy policy.
  • Firebase: Used for backend services, analytics, and crash reporting.

We do not share your personal information with any other third parties.

4. Your Choices

  • You can disable notifications and exact alarms at any time in your device settings.
  • You can opt out of personalized ads via your device's ad settings.
  • Uninstalling the app will remove all locally stored data.

5. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted within the app or on this page.

6. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this privacy policy, please contact us at support@aquatime.app.
